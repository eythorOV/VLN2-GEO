Our Story

It all starting on a monday morning when our team of 3 people decided to start a pizza business. We had no idea what we were doing but we were determined to make it work. We started by making a list of all the things we needed to do and then we started working on them one by one. We created Diagrams and charts to help us visualize our progress and to help us keep track of what we needed to do to start the business. In May of 2023 we were finally ready to open our website to users and the rest is history.

We are now the biggest pizza company in the world and we are still growing.
