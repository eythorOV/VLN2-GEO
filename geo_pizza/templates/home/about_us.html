Our Story

Welcome to GEO Pizzahouse, the brainchild of three lifelong friends with a shared passion for delectable pizzas and a dream to bring their culinary creations to the world. Our story began on a cozy evening, as we gathered around a kitchen counter, kneading dough and experimenting with flavors. Through countless trial and error, we developed a secret recipe that soon became the talk of the town. Fuelled by our ambition, we decided to turn our culinary prowess into a unique online experience. Combining our love for pizzas with our fascination for the world, GEO Pizzahouse was born. Join us at GEO Pizzahouse, where friendship, flavor, and exploration converge to create an unforgettable pizza experience.
